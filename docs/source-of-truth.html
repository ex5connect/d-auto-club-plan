Source of Truth & Team Roles

Business Source of Truth

Design Team

More than UI design, they shape how the product works.

  • Whole app UI plus the business flow behind it, all theirs.
  • They set the business direction. How a feature works, just follow their designs.
  • Figma is the reference. Plans and docs follow the design, not the other way round.
  • Not sure how something should work? Go check with the design team first.
In short: when frontend or backend finds the design and the docs don't match, or something is unclear, talk to the design team first, then build it together.

Supporting Role

PM

Really just the glue. Here to support, not to decide.

  • Glue for every process: connect people, keep things moving.
  • Make sure the timeline stays on track.
  • Sort out whatever resources the team needs, clear the blockers.
  • More like a collaborator / consultant. Give input, never override.

Every Team

Make it shine, and shine with it

This project is our showcase. Everyone included, no matter the role.

  • Your work is your signature. Ship stuff you're proud of.
  • Share your ideas, even outside your own role. Good ideas can come from anyone.
  • Got a question? Ask early. One question today saves one rework next month.
  • Help each other across roles. We've got each other's backs, nobody shines alone.
  • Small details matter. That's what makes the app feel great to use.
  • When the project shines, everyone who built it shines too.

Give it your best. Make it shine, make yourself proud.

Contribute more, speak up more, own your part. One team, one goal.
